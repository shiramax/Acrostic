Insert Words Or Sentences

You insert the words or the sentences you want to remember

Collect The First Letter

We collect from each word or sentence you entered the first letter in the line.
Now, we have a new string from these letters. We will order this string by lexicographic order.
For example, you entered the following words: Milk, Donuts, Hot Dogs, Rice, Apples, Honey, Onions, Tomatos, Eggs and Olives.
The initial string we will get is: (M,D,H,R,A,H,O,T,E,O). Finally, we will get this lexicographic sorted string: dehhmooort.

Our Magic

Now, once we have created a string sorted by lexicographic order it's time for us to start our MAGIC.
Our database is based on both english dictionary of Oxford and approximately 200 books. In order to find the word / sentence that will provide a perfer ACROSTIC for your wish we sorted our DataBase exactly like we sorted the string.

YOUR ACRISTIC IS READY

Now with the use of a simple query we will find you a sutiable ACROSTIC!

Gat help from our growing community

Unfortunatly we couldn't find you an ACROSTIC in our database, but that's exactly why we have created our community.
You can post a request for Acrostic and our community will help you to find an Acrostic with creative and cool suggestions.
We want you to know that you can post in our community as you want, even before getting the system's Acrostic
